How to Play

Welcome to the Color Game! Test your luck, trust your instincts, and win big by choosing the right color.

🎨 Game Objective

Predict which color will appear. Earn money based on your bet.

💰 Starting Money

You start the game with ₱100. Use this money to place bets on your chosen color.

🎯 Instructions

  • Enter the amount of money you want to bet. Make sure you have enough balance.
  • Select one color from the available six colors.
  • Click the Play to start and will generate a rondom of 3 colors.
  • If your chosen color match with the random 3 colors, you win. if doesn't you lose your bet.

🧮 Winning Rules

Your winnings depend on how many times your color appears

  • 1 match → Win 1x your bet
  • 2 matches → Win 2x your bet
  • 3 matches → Win 3x your bet and have a chance to win more with 5 to 100 times of your bet

ℹ️Additional

  • It can be play with a group of people play it with 2 minutes. After 2 minutes the one who has more money will win.
  • In Transactions: You can borrow 500 and you will get 500 debt
  • In Transactions: You can pay your debt from your bank money
  • In Transactions: You can cash out and transfer money to your bank money
  • In Transactions: You can cash in and transfer money from your bank money
  • In Transactions: You can restart the game using restart button. It will remove the data you played

⚠️ Tips

  • Bet wisely higher bets mean higher risks.
  • Don't go all-in every round.
  • Luck changes—play smart!
